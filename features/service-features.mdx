## Security & Privacy

<Accordion title="SSL Encrypted Transmission" icon="lock">
All document uploads and transmissions use verified SSL certificates.

- 256-bit encryption during transfer
- Secure connection from your browser to our servers
- Protected against interception

Your documents are as secure as online banking.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Automatic Document Deletion" icon="trash">
Documents are automatically deleted from PayPerFax servers within 30 days.

**Why 30 days?**
- Allows time for dispute resolution
- Provides confirmation/receipt access
- Meets data retention compliance

After 30 days, all traces are permanently removed.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="No Account Data Collection" icon="eye-slash">
Since PayPerFax doesn't require registration:
- No login credentials stored
- No password databases
- No long-term profile information
- Minimal personal data collected

Only transaction-necessary information is retained temporarily.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Payment Security" icon="credit-card">
Payment processing handled by trusted providers:
- **Stripe** for credit cards
- **PayPal** for PayPal payments

PayPerFax does not store your credit card numbers or payment details.

## Reliability Features

<Accordion title="Automatic Retry System" icon="rotate">
If your fax fails on the first attempt, PayPerFax automatically retries up to 3 times. This handles temporarily busy lines or offline fax machines.

<Info>
You're only charged once, even if multiple attempts are made.
</Info>

[Learn more about retries](/troubleshooting/resending-faxes)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Real-Time Tracking" icon="chart-line">
Monitor your fax status in real-time through the web interface:
- **Queued**: Waiting to send
- **Sending**: Currently transmitting
- **Delivered**: Successfully sent
- **Failed**: Could not deliver (after 3 attempts)

No need to wonder if your fax went through.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Email Confirmations" icon="envelope">
Receive detailed email notifications when your fax completes:
- **Success**: Confirmation with transmission details
- **Failure**: Notification explaining why it failed

Includes:
- Date and time
- Destination details (CSID)
- PDF copy of transmitted fax
- Transaction details
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Fine Quality Transmission" icon="image">
Faxes are sent at fine quality: **204×196 dpi**

This is the standard resolution for fax transmissions worldwide, ensuring compatibility with all fax machines.
</Accordion>

## What PayPerFax is best for

<Accordion title="Ideal use cases" icon="thumbs-up">
✅ **Occasional fax senders** - Send 1-10 faxes per month
✅ **One-time faxing needs** - Don't need regular service
✅ **Personal use** - Medical forms, legal documents, etc.
✅ **Small businesses** - Infrequent fax requirements
✅ **International faxing** - Send to 130+ countries
✅ **No-commitment preference** - Don't want subscriptions
