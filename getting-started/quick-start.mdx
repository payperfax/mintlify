Get your fax sent in minutes. No registration or software installation required.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A web browser with internet access
- The document you want to fax (PDF, DOC, DOCX, TIF, or JPEG)
- Destination fax number with country code
- Credit card or PayPal account for payment

## Send your first fax

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload your document">
    Visit [app.payperfax.com](https://app.payperfax.com)

    1. Select the destination country by clicking the flag
    2. Enter the fax number with area code
    3. Enter your email address (for confirmation)
    4. Optionally add a cover page
    5. Upload your document (max 8MB)

    <Info>You must provide either a cover page or an attached file (or both) to proceed.</Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Preview and confirm pricing">
    Click **"Preview fax & check price"**

    - Review what your fax will look like
    - See the guaranteed final price
    - Processing takes 30-60 seconds

    <Tip>The price you see here is the final price - no hidden fees or surprises!</Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pay and send">
    Click **"Continue to payment"**

    Choose your payment method:
    - Credit card (enter details directly)
    - PayPal (click through to approve)

    Your fax will be sent immediately after payment authorization.

    <Warning>
    Your card won't be charged immediately. A hold is placed for the amount, and you're only charged if the fax succeeds.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Track your fax" icon="chart-line">
    After payment, you'll see a tracking page showing real-time status:
    - **Queued**: Fax is waiting to be sent
    - **Sending**: Fax is being transmitted
    - **Delivered**: Fax sent successfully
    - **Failed**: Fax could not be delivered (no charge)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Receive confirmation" icon="envelope">
    When your fax completes (success or failure), you'll receive an email with:
    - Date and time of transmission
    - Destination fax identifier (CSID)
    - PDF copy of the actual fax sent
    - Final charge status
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="If your fax fails" icon="shield">
    Don't worry! If your fax fails:
    - You won't be charged
    - The payment hold will be released
    - PayPerFax automatically retries 3 times
    - You'll receive a notification about the failure
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
